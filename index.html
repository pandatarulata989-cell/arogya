Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the consultation fee?

Our standard consultation fee is very affordable at just ₹300. Treatment session charges may vary depending on the therapy required.

What are the clinic timings?

We are open daily and provide services until 7:00 PM. Please book an appointment in advance to minimize waiting time.

How many physiotherapy sessions are needed?

The number of sessions depends on your specific condition, severity of the pain, and how your body responds to the treatment. Dr. Padhi will provide an estimated recovery plan after your initial assessment.

Do you treat accident recovery patients?

Yes, we specialize in post-accident rehabilitation, helping patients regain strength, mobility, and independence after traumatic injuries or surgeries.

Is physiotherapy painful?

Physiotherapy is designed to relieve pain, not cause it. While you may experience mild discomfort during certain stretching or strengthening exercises, we ensure treatments are within your tolerance level.
